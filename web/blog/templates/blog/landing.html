About

In my day job, I spend a lot of time working with numbers and data. As of the time I'm writing this, I work mostly remote and don't get very many opportunities to express myself other than presenting or collaborating on projects at work. All that to say, it's easy to burn out if you don't have an outlet.

About a year ago I started to journal in a Google Spreadsheet on the different projects that I was working on outside of work. I found that having a place where I could organize my thoughts was relaxing in that it served as a creative outlet but also provided some much needed organization. Around that same time, I was getting more involved with Web Development type projects in my day job. Fast forward to the beginning of 2021 and having already built several websites, I realized it was time to build a site that could house that Google Spreadsheet of thoughts and ideas. This is that website.

There's over a billion something websites currently in existence and time is valuable so the fact that you are on my sliver of the web is something I'm grateful for. If you'd like to get in touch, there's a form at the bottom of this page you can use to contact me. My work (and content) centers mostly around Linux, Python (Django), Javascript (React), C++, PostgreSQL, Cloud/DevOps type things, systems architecture, and software development in general. If that sounds like something I can help you with or you'd like to talk about feel free to reach out.
